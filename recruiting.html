Recruiting
If you would like to join the UMD Women’s Club Crew Team, please follow the directions below:
1) Sign up on IMLeagues AND TerpLink
1.1) IMLeagues: https://www.imleagues.com/spa/portal/home
1.1.1) Click the login button
1.1.2) Search for the University of Maryland
1.1.3) Log in with your UMD ID and password
1.1.4) Search for “Club Crew - Women”
1.1.5) Request to join our team
1.1.6) Once you’ve been added to the roster, you MUST fill out the “Club Participant’s Personal Information” AND schedule a time to conduct a swim test
1.2) TerpLink: https://terplink.umd.edu/
1.2.1) Click “Sign In”
1.2.2) Log in with your UMD ID and Password
1.2.3) Search for the “Maryland Women’s Crew Team” and click the link to our organization
1.2.4) Request to join our team
2) Email our recruitment chair - umdwomenscrewrecruitment@gmail.com
In you email, please include the following:
  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • UID
  • Detailed description of any and all previous experience with rowing. If no experience, just write that you are new to the sport (NOTE: It is ok if you have no experience! Newcomers are always welcome and we will teach you everything you need to know)
  • Statement acknowledging that you have signed up on both IMLeagues and TerpLink
  • Date of scheduled swim test
  • Any questions you may have
